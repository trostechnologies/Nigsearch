Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)

The Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) is a regulation on data protection and privacy for all individuals or natural persons resident in Nigeria. If you are a Nigerian resident, we are Data Controllers of your data and you have the right to know how your personal information is processed, what we collected about you and to ask that this data to be corrected, updated, or deleted. Additionally, please note that your information may be transferred outside of Nigeria for processing. Please contact us if you have any questions about your NDPR rights. For more information or complaints please contact your local data protection authority.
