About Our Company

Why use an IT Managed Service Provider?

Nigsearch.com Limited is a global IT managed Service Provider. Our main focus is to deliver high value to clients through reliable integrated flexible and cost effective solutions. We have delivered our solutions to Small, Medium and Large companies within FTSE 1000 companies in the UK and Nigeria for over two decades enabling businesses to overcome key business challenges. We manage most of the IT services for our clients, which includes Application Systems, Security and IT Infrastructure. Our services are within the parameters agreed with the client based on the Service Level Agreement (SLA).
Our Services alleviates the work load of Internal IT department for them to carry on Business as usual increasing their productivity. We blend industry best practices with corporate culture to ensure financial responsibility creating a well deserved ROI. Our operational framework combines Business, Technology and most importantly Finance to accelerate corporate value and promote financial accountability. We gauge our customer successes and regard it as a privilege to ensure 100% customer satisfaction.
